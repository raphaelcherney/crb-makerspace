# Hand and Power Tools

The CRB Makerscapce is equipmented with a wide variety of hand tools. Most are available in labeled drawers. If you need help finding a tool, ask the Makerspace Manager.

Equipment includes:

- Drill / Driver
- Saws
- Screw and hex drivers
- Files
- Cutting tools
- Wrenches and sockets


## Hazard level

<span style="background-color: orange; color: white; padding: 4px 8px; border-radius: 4px; font-size: 2.2em; font-weight: bold;">MEDIUM</span>

!!! warning

    - Risk of cuts, lacerations, and flying debris
    - Rotating parts can cause entanglement
    - Risk of kickback and crush injuries
    - Risk of electric shock from damaged cords
    - High noise levels can damage hearing


## Safety

- Always wear eye protection when using tools.
- Always mount your workpiece when cutting or drilling.
