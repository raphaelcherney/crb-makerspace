# Electronics

## Workbenches

There are two workbenches for use. Each workbench should be equiped with the following:

### Core tools

- Work mat
- Flush cutters
- Wire strippers
- Tweezers
- Needle-nose pliers
- Soldering iron with stand & tip cleaner (sponge or brass wool)
- Fume extractor
- Desoldering pump (solder sucker)
- Helping hands
- Safety glasses

### Instruments

- Digital multimeter (Brymen BM235)
- Bench power supply (Keithley 2231A-30-3)
- Oscilloscope (Rigol DS1054Z and Tektronix MSO 2012B)
- Microscope (Nikon SMZ-U)
- Solder stations (Metcal MX-500P and Matcal MFR-PS1300)
- Hot air rework station (Aoyue 852)

### Consumables

- Solder (spool)
- Flux pen and/or liquid flux
- Isopropyl alcohol (IPA)
- Disposable swabs
- Desoldering braid
- Hookup wire
- Kapton tape
- Electrical tape

## Components and Materials

The lab also has a variety of electronics components and materials for use, including:

- Resistors
- Capacitors
- Jumper wires
- Heat shrink tubing
