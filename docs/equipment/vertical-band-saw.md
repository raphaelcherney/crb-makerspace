# Vertical Band Saw

![Vertical band saw](../assets/vertical-band-saw.jpg){ width="400" }

The CRB Makerspace has a Powertec BS900 9" vertical band saw to cut plastics and non-ferrous metals.

## Hazard level

<span style="background-color: red; color: white; padding: 4px 8px; border-radius: 4px; font-size: 2.2em; font-weight: bold;">HIGH</span>

!!! warning

    - Risk of severe cuts and amputations from saw blade
    - Risk of workpiece kickback or binding
    - Pinch points between blade and table
    - Blade breakage may cause flying metal fragments
    - Cut metal parts may get hot and cause burns
    - Risk of inhalation of plastic/metal dust

## Safety

- Always wear safety glasses.
- Do not wear gloves, neckties, loose clothing, or jewlery.
- Adjust the upper guide to just clear of workpiece.
- Handle workpiece correctly. Hold workpiece firmly against the table. Use push stick or push blocks when needed.
- Round stock can get caught and spin. Prevent rotation or use the horizontal bandsaw (with vise) instead.
- Keep fingers safe distance from blade path. Do not push with your fingers in line with the blade.

## Instuctions for use

!!! info
    
    Use of this tool requires permission from management and a key to operate. Check out the key from Raphael or authorized personnel before use and return it immediately after.

!!! failure "Do not cut steel in the CRB Makerspace"

- Always use eye protection.
- Lower blade guide to adjust above the intended workpiece.
- Insert the key and turn on the machine.
- Feed steadily.  **No fingers in line with blade.** Use push sticks for small parts.
- No backing out while running. Stop the machine first or you risk pulling out a moving blade.
- Turn off the machine and return the key.
- Clean up your workspace.
